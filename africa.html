Tools utilized: ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator

Created: Spring 2021

Client/Audience: Introduction to Cartography course at UW–Madison (Geography: 370)


I created Africa: General Reference with a Physical Focus for a lab in UW-Madison’s Introduction to Cartography course. All the data came from various Natural Earth data sets. I hand placed all the labels over a couple of weeks while either sitting at the Bean and Creamery or in my dorm room in Slichter residence hall.
