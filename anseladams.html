Tools utilized: ESRI StoryMaps, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, Adobe Illustrator

Created: September-October 2021

Client/Audience: Graphic Design in Cartography course at UW–Madison (Geography: 572)


I created Discs, Diversity, & Discourse: Reflections on the PDGA and the greater Disc Golf community as a project for UW-Madison’s Graphic Design in Cartography course after picking up disc golf during the second summer of the pandemic at a short nine (8) basket course a few blocks from my house. Built in Esri StoryMaps, Discs, Diversity, & Discourse is a look into and an overview of the rise in discourse, in recent years, surrounding diversity and the lack there of in the Disc Golf community. Based around a collection of articles I collected that range from a semi-pro’s op-eds to an interview with professional Disc Golf player to a multitude of official and volunteer gathered research surrounding the demographics of the sport.
