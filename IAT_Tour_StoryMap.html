Tools utilized: ESRI StoryMaps, ArcGIS Online

Created: November-December 2021

Client/Audience: Ice Age Trail Alliance (IATA) and Final Project for Graphic Design in Cartography course at UW–Madison (Geography: 572)


The Explore the Ice Age National Scenic Trail StoryMap is a combination project that I created both for a final project in my Graphic Design in Cartography course at UW–Madison as well as for the Ice Age Trail Alliance for use on the organization’s website. This StoryMap was based off an earlier version that was missing a handful of the trail’s total segments due to past constraints within StoryMaps at the time of its creation. However, this updated StoryMap was built up from scratch to increase the projects livability and includes changes in layout, design, and overall content from the original version. The edits and additions to this new Ice Age National Scenic Trail Tour StoryMap was completed in collaboration with the Ice Age Trail Alliance’s Director of Marketing and Community Relations and their GIS and Technology Specialist.
